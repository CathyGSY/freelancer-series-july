03 · Cassie

Cassie

4 videos · FX / Ecom
Direction: FX / Ecom  ·  Tone: savvy seller who found the smarter way, not a finance lecturer  ·  Products: World Account, multi-currency collection, Pay-to-China
Context. FX content performs when it makes an invisible cost visible. Most ecom sellers don't think about currency conversion as a margin problem — they think about product cost, shipping, ads. The job of Cassie's content is to reframe: the FX rate your bank applies to every Amazon payout or supplier payment is a hidden tax you're paying every single time. The best-performing examples use concrete dollar amounts, not percentages. "4% FX fee" feels abstract. "Banks took $4,000 of my $100,000 payout last quarter" lands.
Amazon / TikTok Shop sellers cross-border ecom operators international freelancers FX SAVING
Key insight. The best viral FX content on TikTok uses the "hidden cost" reveal, not the "great product" pitch. Show the fee FIRST. Make the viewer feel the loss. Then show the solution. The woodentoyjay post (9,928 likes) is the clearest benchmark: "banks are eating 4–5% of my profit" → product as the fix. Cassie should own this frame across all four videos with different contexts (Amazon payouts, supplier payments, freelancer collection, multi-platform sellers).
