Live audit of all published content (popular sort, ~45 TikTok videos + Instagram grid). Use this to avoid duplicating what's already been done, and to find the real gaps. Audited 2026-06-26.

What the top 3 have in common
1. Dollar stakes in the hook
"I watched a guy lose $50,000 to a fake supplier." Not "how to spot fake suppliers." The number is in the first 2 seconds. Generic hooks get skipped.
2. Save-worthy, not share-worthy
5–6% save rate on both top videos. Comments are low. People bookmark these as reference guides. Comments like "Just saved to come back to it" and "Best insight ever." Format = practical guide, not entertainment.
3. Specific actionable steps
Not awareness — "here are the 6 apps that will save your China trip." Numbered, concrete, executable. Audiences save content they plan to use again.
